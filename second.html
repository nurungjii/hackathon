HOVER OVER THE APPLES ON THE TREE TO SEE SOME INFORMATION ABOUT WHY TREES ARE BENEFICIAL!

-releases oxygen for us to breathe

-absorbs carbon dioxide and other toxins and allergens from the air

-functions as sound barriers that reduce noise pollution

-provides shade and prevents the build up of heat during the day

-produces fruits, nuts, and lumber

-trees in forests filter rainwater which help fight water pollution

-absorbs dangerous chemicals from surrounding soils through a process called phytoremediation

-as fallen tree leaves decay, it reduces soil moisture loss and provides nutrients for tree growth
